Overview

The short version

Taqwa collects only what it needs to run the features you use — your account details when you sign in, your device location to calculate prayer times and the Qibla, and the activity you choose to record (completed goals, dhikr counts, bookmarks, and community posts).

We never sell your data, show third-party advertising, or track you across other apps. If you use Taqwa as a guest, nothing you do is saved to our servers at all.

Data

Information we collect

We only collect data tied to features you actually use. Here is everything, by category.

Category What it includes When
Account Your name, email address, and profile photo, provided by Google when you sign in. Only if you sign in
Location Your device's geographic coordinates, and an optional location label you can save to your profile. When you grant the location permission
Worship activity Completed daily goals, dhikr (zikr) counts, prayer streaks, Surah bookmarks, and focus habits you track. While signed in
Preferences Calculation method, prayer-reminder and adhan-sound toggles, and theme (light/dark). While signed in / on device
Community Posts, comments, likes, joins, and shares you create in the community feed. When you post or interact

What we do not collect

  • No advertising identifiers and no cross-app tracking.
  • No contacts, photos library, microphone, or call/SMS data.
  • No continuous or background location — coordinates are read only while the app is open and you've granted permission.
Purpose

How we use your information

  • Prayer times & Qibla — your location is used to compute accurate prayer times and the Qibla direction. This calculation happens on your device.
  • Your account — your name and photo personalise the app; your email identifies your account so your data syncs across sessions.
  • Progress & streaks — your goal and dhikr logs let the app show your daily progress and compute your current and longest streaks.
  • Community — content you post is shown to other signed-in members of the Taqwa community.
  • Reminders — if you enable prayer reminders, we send local notifications from your device.
Permissions

Device permissions we request

Taqwa asks for these permissions only for the purpose described, and the app keeps working (with limited features) if you decline.

Location Notifications Internet

Location

Used to calculate prayer times and the Qibla for where you are. If you decline, Taqwa falls back to a default location and you can still use the app.

Notifications

Used to deliver the prayer reminders you opt into. You can turn these off in the app or your device settings at any time.

Third parties

Who we share data with

We do not sell your personal data, and we share it only with the service providers that make the app function:

  • Supabase — our backend and database host, which stores your account, activity, and community data securely on our behalf.
  • Google Sign-In — used to authenticate you. We receive your name, email, and photo; we do not receive your Google password.

Community content you choose to post is visible to other signed-in users of Taqwa. We may disclose data if required by law, but we will never share it for advertising or marketing.

Security

How your data is stored & protected

Your data is stored in our Supabase backend and transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections. Database access is protected by row-level security: each record is scoped to its owner, so your private profile and activity are only ever readable by you.

Reference content — like the 99 Names, Surahs, and dhikr catalogue — is read-only and shared. Your personal logs, profile, and bookmarks are private to your account.

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, the associated profile and activity records are removed.

Guest

Using Taqwa without an account

You can browse Taqwa as a guest without signing in. In guest mode the app stores nothing on our servers — your goal completions and dhikr counts live only in the current session, and you cannot post to the community. Prayer times and the Qibla still work, since they're computed on your device.

Control

Your choices and rights

  • Access & correction — view and edit your profile details from the Profile screen in the app.
  • Delete your account — request deletion of your account and associated data; once deleted it cannot be recovered.
  • Withdraw permissions — revoke location or notification access at any time in your device settings.
  • Sign out — sign out at any time to stop syncing data to your account.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

Children

Children's privacy

Taqwa is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will remove it.

Updates

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. When we make material changes, we'll revise the "Last updated" date at the top and, where appropriate, notify you in the app. Continued use of Taqwa after an update means you accept the revised policy.
