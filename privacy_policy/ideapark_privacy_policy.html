Privacy Policy for IdeaPark

Last Updated: May 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to IdeaPark. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect and Device Permissions

To provide you with a frictionless experience for capturing and organizing your ideas, IdeaPark requests specific device permissions. We only access this data when you actively use the related features:

  • Microphone (Audio Record): We request access to your device's microphone to allow you to capture voice notes and audio ideas. Audio data is used strictly for transcription and storage within your own account.
  • Camera: We request camera access so you can take photos directly within the app and attach them to your notes. We do not access your camera in the background.
  • Storage & Media (Photos/Images): We request read access to your device's storage and media library so you can upload existing photos or documents from your gallery to your ideas. We only access the specific files you choose to upload.
  • Internet: We require internet access to securely sync your ideas to the cloud, allowing you to access them across your devices, and to facilitate secure account authentication (such as Google Sign-In).

3. Account Information

When you create an account, we collect basic profile information (such as your email address and name) provided by your authentication provider. This is used solely to maintain your account and secure your data.

4. Data Security & Storage

Your ideas, audio files, and images are stored securely. We do not sell your personal information, notes, media, or audio recordings to third parties. Your data is strictly used to provide the IdeaPark service to you.

5. Third-Party Services

We use secure third-party services for database hosting and authentication. These services are bound by strict data processing agreements and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.
