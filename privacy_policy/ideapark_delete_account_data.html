Delete Your IdeaPark Account and Data

Last Updated: May 4, 2026

At IdeaPark, you own your ideas. If you decide to leave, you have the right to completely and permanently delete your account and all associated data.

How to Delete Your Account (In-App)

The fastest way to delete your data is directly within the IdeaPark app:

  1. Open the IdeaPark app and log into your account.
  2. Navigate to the Settings or Profile menu.
  3. Scroll down and tap on "Delete Account".
  4. Confirm your decision.

How to Request Data Deletion (Web/Email)

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request manual deletion of your account and data:

  1. Send an email to [Insert Your Support Email Address] from the email address associated with your IdeaPark account.
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request".
  3. In the body of the email, state that you wish to permanently delete your account and all stored data.
  4. Our support team will process your request and permanently wipe your data within [e.g., 7 business days], and send you a confirmation email.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

  • Permanent Removal: All of your text notes, voice recordings, and uploaded images will be permanently erased from our secure database.
  • Account Closure: Your profile and authentication links (e.g., Google Sign-In association) will be severed.
  • No Recovery: This action is irreversible. Once deleted, your ideas cannot be recovered.
